ℹ️ About WanderWise

Your trusted travel companion for exploring Delhi safely.

🧭 Our Mission

WanderWise is a community-powered platform designed to help travelers navigate Delhi safely. We aggregate real-time scam reports, provide transparent price data, and offer location insights to ensure you travel smart and stay risk-free.

✨ Features

  • Real-time risk incident reporting and browsing
  • Location-based safety insights for 10+ Delhi areas
  • Taxi fare estimator with scam-prevention tips
  • Food & service price comparisons
  • Community-powered incident database
  • Transport and hotspot information per area

⚠️ Safety Tips for Delhi

  • Always use Ola, Uber, or metered autos — never negotiate fixed rates
  • Book hotels via official websites or trusted OTAs only
  • Be skeptical of strangers offering unsolicited help at monuments
  • Prefer UPI payments to avoid counterfeit currency
  • Keep your bags in front in crowded metro coaches
  • Check bills carefully before paying at restaurants
